My name is Antuan(Tuan). I'm a Computer Science major at Stanford University (2018) pursuing a concentration in Information.

As a technology enthusiast, I'm deeply interested in learning how technology can improve the lives of those in post-Communist economies. I led an effort to establish Idea-Cuba, an exchange program between Cuban and Stanford college students to collaborate on solutions to social issues using technology.

I'm passionate about the growth of entrepreneurship in my home country, Cuba. I'm currently partnering with Cuba-Emprende Foundationto expose winners of 10x10kCuba to Stanford and the Sillicon Valley. I aspire to turn my knowledge and skills into opportunities for my people in Cuba.

I'm also interested in philanthropy, particularly in finding the most effective ways to give. I currently serve on the Student Advisory Board of Giving Circles Fund, an organization led by Laura Arrillaga. I also lead the One Life One Hope circle to help Syrian victims of war.
